## Overview

Graphic design is the art and practice of creating visual content to communicate messages.

## Key Features

- **Visual Communication**: Primary focus is on effectively communicating a message through visual means.
- **Aesthetics**: Emphasizes creating visually appealing and engaging designs.
- **Brand Identity**: Helps establish and maintain a consistent brand image.
- **Versatility**: Applicable across a broad spectrum of industries and platforms.
